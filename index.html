Experience & Journey

With over a decade working in the community pharmacy industry, I've always had a passion for finding the root cause and treating issues from there.

My journey into genetic counselling came from a desire to understand the deeper "why" behind health conditions. I completed my master's research in migraine genetics with the leading genetic migraine research facility. Through this work, I inadvertently reduced the severity and frequency of my own migraines, overcame burnout, and significantly improved my overall health and balance.

Now, I want to help others achieve the same transformation. I take a wholistic approach, considering lifestyle, diet, and vitamins first. While I'm not afraid to recommend pharmaceuticals when needed, they're always considered as a last resort after exploring all other avenues.
